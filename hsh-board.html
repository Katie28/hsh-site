H. Steve Harrell, II

Chairman | Chief Executive Officer

H. Steve Harrell, II will serve as the Chairman of our Board of Directors, President and Chief Executive Officer. Mr. Harrell served as the Chairman of the Board and General Partner of Harrell-Swatty, an Atlanta Georgia based automotive group since 2002. He will continue to serve in this capacity after the consummation of the offering. Since that time, he has been involved with the management of a chain of 14 dealerships with assets of $1 billion. He has been a partner or manager of 20 real estate developments throughout the United States and the Bahamas. He has been a director of the nonprofit Begin Again Ministries with the mission of feeding the homeless for the last five years. Mr. Harrell, II holds a BS in Management from Northwood University.

Thomas Boynton

Board Member | Chief Operating Officer

Thomas Boynton will serve as a Director on our Board, Chief Operating Officer (COO) for HSH and Executive Vice President. During his career, he has served in several key positions at various companies involved in real estate, site development, manufacturing and infrastructure. Mr. Boynton joined Valencia Capital in 2007 and has served as its President since January 2009. Other key positions have included President of his own Utility Infrastructure Construction Group for over 17 years, and Executive Vice President / COO for a small manufacturing company. He has been extensively involved in real estate development focusing heavily on infrastructure and renewables as it relates primarily to resort development. Over the last ten years he has worked heavily in the financial sector to position, structure and raise capital via private equity and various funds for companies and projects to meet their capital requirements. It is this career diversity that affords him the ability understand and comprehend corporate and project operations from every aspect. Mr. Boynton earned his BS in Agricultural Engineering from the University of Georgia in 1984 and is a veteran of the US Navy.

Julius Erving

Director

Julius Erving will serve as a director of the company. Mr. Erving, is a world renown Hall of Fame basketball forward, known globally as Dr. J. In his post basketball career he has worked as a NBC Television Network sports analyst and an executive for the Orlando Magic. He has made his mark in the business community as President of Dr. J Enterprises, a holding company for businesses that he owns. Mr. Erving has also been a public company Board Member and remains active with the NBA. Mr. Erving earned his Bachelor’s degree from the University of Massachusetts Amherst in 1986.
